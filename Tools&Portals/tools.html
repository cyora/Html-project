LogMeIn Rescue


With LogMeIn Rescue, start a remote support session in one click on a mobile device, tablet or computer. You can support your clients on various platforms, including iOS, Android, Mac, Windows and Linux. Easily retrieve system information, control devices remotely, send device configurations.

WISE


Wise is our super powerful tool. It gives you the access to all manuals that can guide through your troubleshooting steps and makes your mission easier through the Control panel messages document (CPMD), Service manual, and Service cost data list.

Part Surfer


HP PartSurfer provides fast, easy access to service parts information for a wide range of HP Products. By entering the serial number of an HP product this tools provide you the part referances and their repair service range.
